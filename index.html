Design is about the balance of beauty, logic and possibilities. The web designer has to make the project look beautiful, but this is only one of his tasks. First of all, he is a designer and analyst who creates simple and understandable products based on the analysis of user behavior.

What does a web designer do?

A web designer develops a simple and clear structure of a new site with a convenient navigation system and an intuitive user interface, selects fonts, backgrounds, icons, draws a logo, animation and banners. The web designer also improves live sites. To do this, he analyzes the behavior of users on the site, trying to understand what they are missing and why they do not stay on the site or cannot find what they were looking for.

How to become a web-designer

To become a web-designer today you can simply take UI / UX design courses or web-design courses. A web-designer combines both UI and UX. You can also become a web designer after graduating from the graphic design department at a university or college.
